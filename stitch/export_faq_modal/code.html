Password reset email not received

Users occasionally report delays or non-delivery of password reset emails. This guide provides troubleshooting steps for both end-users and support agents.

1. Check the Spam/Junk folder.

2. Verify that the email address matches the account on file.

3. Wait at least 15 minutes for SMTP relay delays.

4. Whitelist noreply@system.com in your security settings.
